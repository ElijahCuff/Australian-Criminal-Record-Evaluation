Search through 27,225,634 criminal and civil court records in Australia, that's around 320 thousand more records than the population of people.

"This website can also be used as a simple JSON api, try a request /cda/search.php?api=true&first=Tim&last=Tam&state=VIC".
records will be displayed in JSON encoded array consisting of the record name, count in the records database and the type of court, civil ( family court, etc ) or criminal ( assault, infringements ).
